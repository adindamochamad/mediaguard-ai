Here is how the pipeline works.

You build one medication list — secured with row-level access in Supabase.

A manual Scan Now, or a scheduled job every six hours, triggers the run.

Nimble crawls live web sources — branching into FDA safety communications, PubMed, and medical news.

Claude reads what was crawled, matches it to your specific drugs, scores severity, and filters noise before anything reaches you.

Duplicate alerts from the same source URL collapse to one notification — so repeat scans do not spam you.

Results are stored in Supabase, then split three ways: a realtime push to your browser, a critical email when severity demands it, and a read-only magic link for caregivers.

From trigger to dashboard, the full path runs in under sixty seconds.
